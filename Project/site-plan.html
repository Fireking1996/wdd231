Dracollum Site Plan

Site Name

Dracollum – A fusion of "Draco" (Latin for dragon) and "collum" (suggesting scroll or collection). This name reflects a mystical archive for fantasy writers. Optional domain: dracollum.org.

Site Purpose

The purpose of Dracollum is to inspire and support fantasy writers by offering creative prompts, worldbuilding tools, and a showcase area for fantasy fiction and lore. It will serve as a digital sanctuary for storytelling.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find inspiration when I’m stuck writing my fantasy novel?
  • Are there any resources to help me design magical systems or fantasy creatures?

Color Schema

  • Fantasy Red (#8b1e3f): Background and thematic base color.
  • Parchment Gold (#ffd29d): Used for headings and accent borders.

Typography

  • Merriweather (serif): Used for main body text to evoke a literary and classic tone.
  • Raleway (sans-serif): Used for headers to provide contrast and clarity.
